ABOUT



I am a junior full-stack developer (in training) with Manchester University's Coding Bootcamp focussing on the languages of HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

After graduating from Salford University in 2013 and subsequently building a career in the ethical investment sector of the Finance industry, I am eager to put my skills and energy to good use in an exciting new field!

And I really do mean GOOD use: the ethos of an employer of mine was "Gathering assets to do good", and I believe this can be applied in many areas. I am gathering new assets (coding skills) and plan to use them to do good! I am partcularly interested in the areas of machine learning, eliminating bias in AI and increasing accesibility accross the technosphere.

I try to approach coding like a detective solving a mystery, and that helps to keep me motivated and engaged in my tasks. I am a fast learner with meticulous attention to detail and a creative flair, qualities I think will serve me well in my career ahead.
